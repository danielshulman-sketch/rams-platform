## Sample Scope of Works Document

### Project: Steel Beam Installation

**Client:** ABC Construction Ltd  
**Location:** 123 High Street, London

---

### Work Description

Installation of structural steel beams on the 3rd floor of the commercial building. The work will involve:

1. **Site Preparation**
   - Establish exclusion zones
   - Set up MEWP access equipment
   - Secure all loose materials

2. **Main Activities**
   - Lift steel beams using approved crane
   - Position beams as per structural drawings
   - Perform hot works for welding connections
   - Install bolted connections where specified

3. **Safety Measures**
   - All personnel to wear full PPE
   - Hot works permit required
   - Fire extinguisher on site
   - Competent person supervision

4. **Quality Control**
   - Visual inspection of all welds
   - Torque verification on bolted joints
   - Dimensional checks against drawings

**Estimated Duration:** 5 working days  
**Personnel Required:** 4 steel erectors + crane operator

---

End of scope document.
