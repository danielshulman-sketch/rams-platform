### Scope of Works

Installation of structural steel beams on the 3rd and 4th floors of the commercial office building. This project involves:

**Phase 1 - Preparation:**
- Establish site exclusion zones
- Set up mobile elevated work platforms (MEWP)
- Secure all loose materials and conduct site safety briefing

**Phase 2 - Main Works:**
- Lift and position steel I-beams using 20-ton mobile crane
- Perform hot works for welding beam connections as per structural drawings
- Install M20 bolted connections where specified
- Apply fire protection coating to exposed steelwork

**Phase 3 - Quality Assurance:**
- Visual inspect all welds for defects
- Conduct torque verification on all bolted joints
- Perform dimensional checks against architectural drawings
- Document all work with photographic evidence

**Safety Requirements:**
- Full PPE mandatory at all times
- Hot works permit required daily
- Fire extinguisher and fire watch on site during welding
- Competent Person (CP) supervision throughout
- Method statements and risk assessments in place

**Estimated Duration:** 10 working days  
**Team Size:** 4 steel erectors + 1 crane operator + 1 CP supervisor
